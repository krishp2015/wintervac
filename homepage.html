--About my Tea Shop.--

Organic Tea Hub is a Nepali Tea brand, where tea comes from eastren part of nepal. Organic Tea Hub, located on Jyatha Street in Thamel, Kathmandu, is a sanctuary for tea enthusiasts seeking high quality sourced teas. They offer a diverse selection , including black, green, oolong, and white varieties, all sourced from the hills of Nepal. The shop emphasizes natural goodness and aroma, ensuring a delightful experience for tea lovers.
